About.

Hi! My name is Matheus "TT" Freire and I am a game QA tester and developer. I graduated in Computer Science, focusing my professional career on software testing.

From 2012 to 2016 I worked as main QA Analyst at Aquiris Game Studio, working close to game designers, producers and developers to deliver great quality games for Steam, iOS, Android and browser environments.

Since 2015, I started developing small games by myself and with friends to improve my programming and game design skills. Now, I'm looking for new challenges and the opportunity to learn even more from experienced developers and designers.
